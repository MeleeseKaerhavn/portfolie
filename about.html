Fun fact

I've moved 2 times in USA and 13 times in Denmark, throughout my 27 year life.
Calculations being:
27 × 12 + 5 = 329
329 ÷ 16 = 20.56
Which means I've stayed approximately 1 year and 8.5 months each place.

Fun fact

In 2000 I was sick with Influenza, that quickly turned into respiratory failure. This admitted me to the hospital on a ventilator and in a coma for 31 days.
As my parents were preparing to have me taken off life support, I woke up, essentially saving myself from terminal extubation.
